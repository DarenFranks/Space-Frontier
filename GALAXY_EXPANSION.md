# Galaxy Expansion - 38 New Locations

## Overview
Added 38 new locations organized into faction territories, creating a rich 7-faction galaxy with progression from safe starting zones to deadly outer regions.

## Location Statistics
- **Total Locations**: 56 (18 existing + 38 new)
- **Stations**: 24
- **Planets**: 12
- **Asteroid Belts**: 15
- **Space/Combat Zones**: 5

## Territory Organization

### NEUTRAL ZONE (Safe Starting Area)
**Danger Level**: 0.05 - 0.1
- Nexus Prime Station (existing - starting location)
- Starlight Waystation
- Tranquil Belt
- Freeport Exchange

**Features**:
- No faction affiliation
- Safe for beginners
- All services available
- Low pirate activity

---

### MERIDIAN COLLECTIVE TERRITORY (Trade & Exploration)
**Danger Level**: 0.1 - 0.4
**Stations**: Aurora Reach, Prosperity Hub, Explorer's Outpost
**Planets**: Eden Prime, Horizon Vista
**Asteroids**: Sapphire Fields, Verdant Belt, Crystal Gardens, Uncharted Expanse
**Space Zones**: Merchant Corridor

**Theme**: Democratic traders focused on commerce and exploration
**Faction Ships**: 
- Meridian Envoy (enhanced cargo cruiser)
- Meridian Pathfinder (advanced scout)

**Faction Components**:
- Meridian Advanced Scanner Suite
- Meridian Efficient Power Core

**Loot Drop Chances**:
- Components: 8% from combat
- Ships: 2% from combat
- Mission Rewards: 15% at 75+ reputation
